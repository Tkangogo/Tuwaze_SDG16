Empowering Features

Everything you need to be an active citizen in Uasin Gishu.

Instant Reporting

Report non-emergency issues like streetlights and vandalism. Attach photos and approximate location for privacy.

Access legal aid, paralegals, and rights info. Know procedures and find help nearby.
